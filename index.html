Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

📋 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use WashClub:

  • Personal information: Full name, Telegram account data (used for authentication)
  • Vehicle information: Car number plate, car model and brand
  • Location data: Your device's location to find nearest car wash branches
  • Device information: Device type, OS version, push notification token

🎯 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate users via Telegram
  • Process and manage car wash bookings
  • Send push notifications about booking status and promotions
  • Show nearby car wash branch locations
  • Manage membership and subscription plans

🔒 3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase cloud infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All data transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

🔗 4. Third-Party Services

WashClub uses the following third-party services:

  • Telegram — for user authentication
  • Supabase — for data storage and real-time updates
  • Google Play Services — for push notifications

📍 5. Location Data

We access your device location only to show nearby car wash branches. Location data is not stored on our servers permanently and is only used during your active session.

🔔 6. Push Notifications

We send push notifications about:

  • Booking confirmations and status updates
  • Membership expiration reminders
  • Promotional offers

You can disable notifications anytime in your device settings.

🤝 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or as described in this policy.

👶 8. Children's Privacy

WashClub is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

📝 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes via the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
